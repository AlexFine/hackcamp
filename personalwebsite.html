Me

I love making things. My drive to code and develop was born out of love for creating and product development. My entrepreneurialism first surfaced in elementry school when I would buy pokimon cards in bulk from target, and then upsell specific cards to other students. Ever since then I've been coding games, search engines, iPhone apps and more.

In addition to my love of making outside the class room, I also have a intrinsic passion for learning. Over the summer of 2015 I attended a startup accelerator and entrepreneurship program at MIT's Sloan School for Entrepreneurship.

When I'm not coding or studying, you can find me exploring trails, sprinting at the track, or playing ping pong with my brother.
